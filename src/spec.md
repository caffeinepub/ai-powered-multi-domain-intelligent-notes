# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Transform the application into an AI-powered multi-domain intelligent notes platform with automatic document classification, legal document analysis, student timetable management, quiz generation, and revision tracking.

**Planned changes:**
- Implement automatic domain classification that analyzes uploaded documents and categorizes them as medical reports, legal documents, or general notes without user selection
- Add legal document analysis capability that extracts and explains key information from contracts, agreements, and legal documents
- Create smart timetable feature for students to manage class schedules, study sessions, and deadlines with calendar view
- Implement automatic quiz generation based on user's notes and learned content
- Add revision reminder system with alerts and revision count tracking
- Enhance AI-powered notes with intelligent summaries and key point extraction
- Remove explicit domain selection UI, presenting unified upload interface
- Refine overall interface for polished, professional appearance with consistent styling and intuitive navigation

**User-visible outcome:** Users can upload medical and legal documents that are automatically classified and analyzed, create AI-enhanced notes, manage their study schedule with a smart timetable, take automatically generated quizzes based on their learning material, and receive revision reminders with tracking - all through a unified, professional interface without manual domain selection.
